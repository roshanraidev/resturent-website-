About us

***We Leave A Delicious Memory For You***

At a fair price, we provide hygienic, high-quality, and delicious cuisine. For almost a decade, we've been providing Nepalese and Indian cuisine. Additionally, we offer banqueting halls for parties and celebrations. We offered excellent customer service where you can enjoy your meal as your own home. We provide a wide range of beverages, including whiskey, beer, wine, cocktails, mocktails, and more. We create a welcoming atmosphere in which you may unwind and spend quality time.
